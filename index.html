SUZANNE MERCHANT

Suzanne Merchant is a second year architecture student at the University of Waterloo.

Her design interests lie primarily in the realms of architecture [any act which transforms an idea to a physical reality], computation, and graphic/print design. She is also very interested in understanding how a material behaves; its strength’s and weaknesses, and how one can use this understanding to fabricate better “objects” regardless of the scale.

An avid rock climber, Suzanne also has a passion for singing and a liking for doodling interesting moustaches.
